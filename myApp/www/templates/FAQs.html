What are the delivery charges?

Delivery for any order amount is free for the super markets we partnered with now, but it is subject to vary. As we keep partnering with more stores, the delivery charge will vary from store to store.

Do I need to take a print out of anything..?

Absolutely not.

How long will the delivery take?

Once the order is confirmed, it would take up till 2 hours for the delivery to be completed. But, we ask the customer to choose the delivery slot while placing the order and according to the chosen delivery slot by the customer delivery will be completed within that time period.

Is it possible for me to change add or remove products after placing an order?

Once the order is confirmed, no changes can be done to the order via app, for any changes you need contact respective merchant store.

Is it possible for you to deliver the groceries at my neighbors or in front of my door if I am not at home?

Yes, we can deliver it to the neighbors as per your instruction either done through a call or mentioned while placing the order. In case of cash/card on delivery the amount will be collected from the neighbor.

Do you do same day delivery and what is same day delivery?

We along with our partnered retailers provide with same day delivery for any order placed before 8 PM. Post which order will be delivered the following day.

Is it possible to cancel an entire order?

Yes, the order can be cancelled on the following situations: 1) If one of the requested products are not available. 2) If order has not been dispatched.

What happens if an item is defected (damaged, leaking or expired) during the time of delivery?

We insist you to check the items during the time of delivery. If incase, the products are defected the same can be returned to respective merchant store.
